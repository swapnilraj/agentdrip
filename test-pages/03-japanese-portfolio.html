Selected work

Projects guided by restraint, material honesty, and the quiet power of empty space.

2025 — residential

A house in Arashiyama

A private residence designed around a single maple tree. Sliding screens frame the garden from every room. The interior uses hinoki wood, plaster walls, and very little else.

2024 — commercial

Nami tea room

A four-seat tea room within a department store. The challenge was creating silence in the middle of noise. Raw concrete, a single window, tatami flooring.

2024 — object

Vessels for Nalata Nalata

A series of five ceramic pourers. Unglazed stoneware, thrown to be slightly uneven. Each one holds exactly one cup of water.

2023 — residential

Apartment in Nakameguro

A small Tokyo apartment stripped to its structure and rebuilt with paper, wood, and light. One room flows into the next without doors.
