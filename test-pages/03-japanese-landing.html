design studio

In stillness, clarity emerges

We create spaces and objects that honor silence. Design that breathes, composed with restraint and guided by the principle of ma.

approach

Less, but with intention

Every element we place earns its position. We begin by removing, not adding. The empty space between objects is as important as the objects themselves.

Our practice draws from the traditions of Japanese interior design, where a room speaks through what it chooses not to include. We bring this sensibility to every project.

services

What we offer

Interior design

Residential and commercial spaces designed around tranquility. We work with natural materials, muted palettes, and careful proportions to create rooms that feel both empty and complete.

Object design

Furniture and vessels that embody simplicity. Each piece is reduced to its essential form, crafted from honest materials that age with grace.
